About Me

Kate Andrie UX/UI DESIGNER

I aim to design digital experiences that are both engaging and visually effective. My approach blends creativity with practical solutions to create experiences that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

As an aspiring UX/UI designer with a background in reputation management and customer care, I've learned a lot about understanding what people really need and want. Creating spaces that are both beautiful and practical is a passion of mine. Quick problem-solving and finding creative solutions that simplify life are my strengths.

Excited to work with clients who value a user-centered approach to design. Let’s team up and create something amazing together!

Skills & Tools

  • User Research

    Conducting surveys, interviews, and creating detailed user personas to deeply understand user needs.

  • Wireframing and Prototyping

    Using Figma, Sketch, Canva, and Miro to bring ideas to life through wireframes and prototypes.

  • High-Fidelity Prototyping

    Creating detailed and interactive prototypes that closely mimic the final product.

  • Visual Design

    Applying color theory, typography, and visual consistency to ensure designs are both functional and visually appealing.

  • Interactive Components

    Designing interactive elements, animations, and transitions to create engaging and dynamic user experiences.

  • Usability Testing

    Planning and conducting usability tests, analyzing results, and iterating designs based on feedback.
